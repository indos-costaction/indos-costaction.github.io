## WG3 Training School

The Working Group 3 (Automated Preprocessing Pipelines) Training School is an
in-person working meeting of the INDoS COST Action (CA24161).

- **When:** A meeting of **2 or 3 days** in **autumn 2026** — the date and length are being
  finalised. **[Pre-register](#pre-registration-intent-to-attend)** to help us choose the date.
- **Where:** Room COLABORA, Espacio Converge, Campus Serrano del CSIC, c/ Serrano 113 posterior, Madrid, Spain
- **Who:** Open pre-registration for registered INDoS members (Working Group 3). Around 20–25 places.

Working Group 3 coordinates the standardization of automated preprocessing across
neuroimaging modalities — with an initial focus on EEG and MEG, drawing on
established MRI practice — toward transparent, reproducible, and interoperable
pipelines for shared data. The Training School advances the Group's three task
forces (training materials, a literature review on preprocessing and provenance,
and an inventory of preprocessing tools and workflows), concludes the Group's
Federated Journal Club, and seeds the deliverables of the first Grant Period.

## Programme

The programme below is the working template; **calendar dates follow the date decision**. In a
**2-day** event the co-working / hacking session is an optional Day-2 afternoon; in a **3-day**
event it becomes a full Day 3 and the concluding wrap-up moves to the end.

### Day 1

- From 09:00 to 09:15 - Welcome and objectives
- From 09:15 to 11:00 - Task Force 3 (Training): Federated Journal Club — concluding session
- From 11:00 to 11:15 - Coffee
- From 11:15 to 13:00 - Task Force 3 (Training): training materials roadmap and next steps
- From 13:00 to 14:00 - Lunch
- From 14:00 to 15:45 - Task Force 2 (Literature review): scope, review pre-registration, modality subgroups
- From 15:45 to 16:00 - Coffee
- From 16:00 to 18:00 - Task Force 2 (Literature review): review protocol and reviewer assignment

### Day 2

- From 09:00 to 10:30 - Task Force 1 (Tools and workflows inventory): scope and taxonomy
- From 10:30 to 10:45 - Coffee
- From 10:45 to 12:15 - Task Force 1 (Tools and workflows inventory): survey design and inventory outline
- From 12:15 to 13:00 - Concluding wrap-up: synthesis, milestones, and next steps *(2-day format)*
- From 13:00 to 14:00 - Lunch
- From 14:00 to 18:00 - Open co-working and hacking session — optional (2-day) or the start of Day 3 (3-day)

### Day 3 *(3-day format only)*

- A full hands-on co-working / hackathon day on the tools-and-workflows inventory, closing with
  the concluding wrap-up.

## Pre-registration (intent to attend)

Attendance is by **open pre-registration** for registered INDoS Action members (Working Group 3),
with around **20–25 places** (we may be able to welcome more if resources permit). The date and
length of the meeting are not yet fixed — the **pre-registration form lets you help choose them**:

- You score several **candidate dates** as *Yes / If need be / No*, and say whether you'd prefer
  a **2-day or 3-day** event.
- Once the organisers pick the final date, **everyone who marked it *Yes* or *If need be* is moved
  from pre-registered to registered** (subject to capacity, in priority order).
- If you didn't pick the chosen date, you can **still register afterwards** — you simply join the
  back of the queue. You can **withdraw** any time before the cancellation deadline.

> **Pre-registration is not a reimbursement guarantee.** It records your intent to attend and is
> separate from e-COST. Reimbursement requires INDoS WG3 membership and an **e-COST invitation**
> that you accept — the local organiser issues these **after** the date is fixed. See
> [Reimbursement](#reimbursement) below. Not yet a member? [Join INDoS](/join) before the event
> to be reimbursable.

## Reimbursement

Participation is reimbursed through the standard COST mechanism. After accepting the
e-COST invitation and attending, submit the Online Travel Reimbursement Request
(OTRR) in e-COST within 30 days of the event. Reimbursement comprises a flat-rate
daily allowance (per the COST daily-allowance table for the host country) plus
travel costs up to a total of €1,500. See the
[COST Annotated Rules](https://www.cost.eu/cost-actions/annotated-rules-qa/) and the
[e-COST platform](https://e-services.cost.eu/) for details.
